Fish Fry Recipes

Ingredients :

  • 4-6 pieces of fish fillets (such as tilapia, cod, catfish, or any white fish of your choice)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional for added flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference, optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Vegetable oil for frying
